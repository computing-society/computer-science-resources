A survey course designed to introduce students to Human-Computer Interaction and prepare them for further study in the specialized topics of their choice. Students will have the opportunity to delve deeper in the course through a course project, and through a two-three week special topic selected at the instructor&#39;s discretion. Course is cross-listed as CSE 323, EST 323 and ISE 323.
