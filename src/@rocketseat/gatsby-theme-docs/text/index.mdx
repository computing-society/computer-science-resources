# Getting started

This is a curated list of free resources for SUNY Korea Computer Science students from SKCS. Visit the course links on the left tab to browse through a course resources you are interested in.

## What is SUNY Korea?

[SUNY Korea](http://sunykorea.ac.kr) was established in March 2012 as the first American university in Korea. It is located in Songdo, and offers
undergraduate and graduate degree programs in Computer Science, Mechanical Engineering, Technological Systems Management, Applied Mathematics and Statistics, and Business Management from [Stony Brook University](https://www.stonybrook.edu) (SBU).

## What is SKCS?

It is a university club founded by Computer Science students. We aim to provide an environment/platform for people passionate in CS to collaborate and develop new projects.

## Disclaimer

Do not use this website as an only guide for your academic planning. Please contact your academic advisor and refer to official documents from the university before taking any decisions. The information on this website is provided as-is and may not be up-to-date.
